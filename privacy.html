Privacy Policy

At quantivest.info, we are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and ensuring the highest level of protection for your personal and financial data. This policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your information when you use our platform.

1. What Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data:

  • Personal details: name, contact number, email, address
  • Banking preferences and account activity on our platform
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, location, usage logs

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information to:

  • Open and manage your financial account
  • Provide tailored investment services
  • Enhance platform security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with financial regulations and legal obligations

3. Data Protection & Security

We use industry-standard security practices including 256-bit SSL encryption, secure data storage, and strict access controls to protect your data.

4. Sharing Your Data

We do not sell your personal information. We may share it with our trusted service providers, who are contractually bound to maintain its confidentiality and security.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal data
  • Request updates or deletion of your information
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

6. Use of Cookies

We use cookies to improve user experience and analyze platform usage. You can control or disable cookies through your browser settings.

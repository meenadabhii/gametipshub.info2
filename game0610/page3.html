Learning About UPI

UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is India's mobile payment system that lets you transfer money between banks using your smartphone.

How it works: Link your bank account to a UPI app (like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) using your mobile number.

UPI ID: Your unique payment address (like name@bankname). Share this to receive money.

MPIN: A 4-6 digit secret code to authorize transactions (never share this!).

Safety Tips for Young Users

Always use with parent's guidance

Never make payments without informing your parents.

Never share your MPIN

Your MPIN is like a password - keep it secret.

Verify before paying

Always check the recipient's details twice.

Use UPI Lite for small amounts

UPI Lite allows payments up to ₹200 without entering MPIN each time.

Check transaction limits

Most student accounts have lower daily limits for safety.

Beware of scams

Never share OTPs or click on payment links from unknown sources.
